Personal Favorites
Lanzhou Noodle Soup: Chinese noodle soup with hand-pulled noodles, a clear beef broth, slices of tender beef, radish, and topped with cilantro.
Miso Ramen: Japanese noodle soup with a miso broth, slices of charsiu, soft-boiled egg, corn, bamboo shoots, enok mushrooms, and topped with seaweed and scallions.
Beef Wellington: One of Gordon Ramsay's famous dishes. Made with a tenderloin filet wrapped in proscuitto, wrapped in a mushroom duxelle (variety of mushrooms finely chopped, sauteed, seasoned, and reduced), and finally wrapped in puffed pastry.
